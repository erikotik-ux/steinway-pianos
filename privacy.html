Last updated: April 26, 2026

Steinway & Sons ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information you provide when visiting this website or submitting an inquiry.

When you submit a Request Information form on this site, we collect:

  • Full name

We do not collect payment information. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely to respond to your inquiry. Specifically, we use it to:

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill your inquiry and any follow-up communication. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
