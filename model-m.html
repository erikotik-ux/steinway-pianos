A century of bringing Steinway into the home.

One of Steinway's most beloved instruments, the Model M has been bringing concert-quality sound into homes and studios for over a century. At 5′ 7″, it offers a compelling balance of tonal richness and spatial practicality — a Steinway that truly fits the way people live.

The Model M's voice is warm and full, with an evenness across all registers that speaks to the care invested in its scale design and voicing. Whether used for serious practice, teaching, or the pleasure of everyday music-making, the Model M rewards with sound that never grows ordinary.

It is the instrument most often recommended to those seeking their first Steinway grand — and it is the instrument that so many pianists choose to keep for a lifetime.
